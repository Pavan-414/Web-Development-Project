Our Certificates & Programs For 2023

Sri Venkateshwaraa Group of Institutions, comprising Indirani College of Nursing, Sri Venkateshwaraa College of Engineering and Technology, and Sri Venkateshwaraa College of Paramedical Sciences,hosted a grand Graduation Day ceremony on the 16th of July 2023.


Ranked with 198 Among Top Engineering Colleges by NIRF 2022, MHRD, Govt. of India. SVCET is also NBA Accredited under Tier-I for three years upto 30.06.2025 and Accredited by NAAC with A Grade Accredited Institution. Established in the year 1998, the Institute is Affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Ananthapuramu and Recognized by AICTE, New Delhi. It is also Recognized by the UGC under Section 2(f) and 12(b). The Institute has been Granted Autonomy by UGC from Academic Year 2011-2012. The Institute offers Eleven(11) UG and Six(6)PG programs in Engineering and Technology, MBA , MCA and offers Four(5) Diploma programms”


The college is located on high way in an area of 65 acres of scenic beauty at R.V.S. Nagar, Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh. The College is located seven kilometers from Chittoor and 60 KM from Tirupathi, the famous hill shrine of Lord Sri Venkateswara.
